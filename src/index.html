What is SynthQuencer

SynthQuencer is a sequencer that is easy to use and does not require the user to have any knowledge of coding or music, just press some buttons and see what happens!

How to get started

Click the link in the header called 'App'. That will take you to SynthQuencer itself.

Once you are there, click 'Start' and the sequencer will start. To play notes, select the notes you want to play in the grid. They will be played once the gray play marker reaches a selected note.
